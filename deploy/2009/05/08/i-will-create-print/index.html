New screenprint from "I will create"

I went along to an exhibition called “Power in Numbers” at the Bodhi Gallery on Brick Lane yesterday. 40 different artists exhibited their prints, each in editions of 50.

Interestingly, all the work was the same size, and the same format. And it was all £20 apart from a cool piece featuring a clock mechanism from Mark McClure. I bought this from I Will Create. Mainly because I thought it was clever. I really like the contrast between the hand-drawn quality of the image, the technicality of the calculator and the funky background pattern. The image is printed in a dark green colour. [PHOTO OF PRINT] Now I just need to find somewhere to put it!

They’ve captured their efforts in printing and hanging in video. If you look carefully on the first video — I’m in the background attending a screen printing workshop that the Print Club runs [LINK TO BLOG POST]
